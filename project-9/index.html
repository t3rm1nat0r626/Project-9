Journey

My journey as a web developer started with my job at amazon and their career choice resource. My wife had showed me the course as we had been looking for a reliable, stable job to work from home to level up our lives so to speak. So once she showed me the video of the intro to the course and what skills we could learn, (also allowing us to live our dream of working from home) we decided to give it a try!
So far in this course I have learned up to intermediate to advanced HTML skills, along with some basic CSS. The most interesting part of all of this to me is that everybody who is learning this course are all working to achieve that same goal of leveling up their lives, along with working from home of course. I also love all of thee styling elements and how you can really make any page you create specifically unique in it's own way.
